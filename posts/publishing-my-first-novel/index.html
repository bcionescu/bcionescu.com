I'm Publishing My First Novel


December 20, 2025

Over the last six months, I’ve been working on my first Sci-Fi novel. If you want to be an ARC reader, you can grab a free copy of it before it goes live on the 2nd of January, 2026.

Here’s the blurb.

Turning the tin around, I can see the name Adam—my name—etched into the back. This is a dangerous thing to do, but it is my way of silently fighting back. If one knows that he is not allowed a name, like an animal in a factory farm, inevitably, he finds one for himself.

Set deep underneath California, centuries after a cataclysmic event, Protectorate is the story of citizen A-376, who is tasked with rooting out a rebel faction that threatens to end the life of everyone he has ever known.

His reward? A life extension which would allow him to live past the age of twenty-eight.
